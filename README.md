# Netflix Content Analysis: Exploring Movie Trends of the 1990s

# README Overview

## Business Problem
A production company specializing in nostalgic entertainment wanted to better understand the characteristics of movies released during the 1990s. By analyzing Netflix's movie catalog, this project explores trends in genres, movie durations, and release patterns that could inform content research and creative decision-making.

---

## Objective
- Analyze movies released in the 1990s.
- Identify common movie durations.
- Explore genre distributions.
- Discover release patterns and content characteristics.

---

## Business Problem

The production company wanted insights into:

- Movie duration trends
- Genre distribution
- Release patterns
- Characteristics of 1990s movies

These insights could support creative research and content strategy.
